Taste of Tate Street

As a third-year student at UNCG, I have tried almost every food option this campus and surrounding area has to offer. While there are many options to choose from I believe Tate Street located near campus has some of the best options avaliable for a cheap price. Below I have listed my top 3 must try place whenever your in the area. Enjoy!
